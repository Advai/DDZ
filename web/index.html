    üéØ Game Flow

    1. Bidding: Players bid 0-3 (or higher based on config). Highest bidder becomes landlord
    2. Landlord Selection: In games with 2+ landlords, highest bidder selects co-landlords
    3. Bottom Cards: Reserved cards are added to landlords' hands
    4. Play: Landlord starts. Players must beat current lead or pass
    5. New Round: After everyone passes, current lead player starts a new round
    6. Victory: First player to empty their hand wins for their team
